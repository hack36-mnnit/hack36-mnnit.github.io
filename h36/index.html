Hack 36 - second Edition

Code the Hack out of it!

MNNITs second edition of Hackathon

Hack 36 is the second edition of Hackathon at MNNIT Allahabad being organised under the aegis of Computer Science and Engineering Department. It will bring together budding hackers and developers for a feast of thirty-six continuous hours. The participants will code solutions to the real-life problems.

Hack 36 will witness hundreds of programmers, developers and hackers from the 26th to the 27th of January, from students all around India who will come together to innovate and invent; forging creations at the forefront of technology. We want you to be a part of this.

Come be a part of Hack 36.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the registration fee?

Registration for the hackathon is free of cost. You won't be charged anything.

What about accomodation and food?

Accomodation and food are absolutely free.

Will travel expenses be reimbursed?

The travel expenses will have to be taken care of by you.

How can I reach Allahabad?

Allahabad is well connected to major cities like Kanpur, Lucknow, Varanasi, Delhi, Jaipur by bus and train.

What can we build?

You can build anything you are passionate about like some web app, mobile app or something using hardware.

Who can apply?

Anyone having a knack of solving real life problems can apply for the hackathon.

How will the shortlisting be done?

Participants will be shortlisted based on their github profile and resume.

What is the procedure of forming a team?

After registration, participants are required to form their team by logging in. We will shortlist teams based on the profiles of the team members.

What should be the size of a team?

A team can consist of 2 to 4 members.

What is the criteria for selection of a team?

If 50% members of a team have decent profile, the team will get shortlisted. In a rare case where a team of 4 members is not shortlisted but 1 member of the team has good profile, that individual member will be shortlisted for the hackathon. That member can form team with other individual members at the event itself.



Come, be a part of Hack 36. Witness the Developers Paradise. Hack 36 awaits your presence.
